The Platform

What ShelterIQ builds

Four interconnected capabilities designed to move shelters from reactive operations to predictive intelligence.

Unified shelter database

A borough-indexed, animal-level data architecture connecting intake, outcome, behavior, medical, foster, and community records across all five shelters — the single source of truth.

Infrastructure

Predictive models

Intake forecasting per shelter, surrender risk scoring, length-of-stay prediction with cross-shelter transfer optimization, and foster conversion models — built with production-grade pipelines.

ML / AI

Real-time dashboards

Live, per-shelter views replacing static reports — tracking intake, placement rates, capacity, and community program effectiveness in a format non-technical staff can act on immediately.

Dashboards

Multi-tenant SaaS platform

Every architectural decision is made with portability in mind. The schema, pipelines, and dashboards built for ACC form the core of a scalable product for municipal shelter systems nationwide.

Scale

Staff-facing decision tools

Model outputs translated into plain-English recommendations. Which animals are at risk? Where should transfers go? What drives foster conversion? Answers staff can use — without needing a data background.

UX

Population dynamics modeling

Simulation tools that model shelter population over time — letting leadership stress-test policies, plan for intake spikes, and measure the systemic impact of community programs before rolling them out.

Research

Our Approach
